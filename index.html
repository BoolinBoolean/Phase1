Hello and welcome to my website. This site is currently a WiP and will eventually have information on multiple different kinds of computer hardware and their many benchmarks as well as some information about them. The main reason I decided to make this website is because when I went through the process of upgrading my own computer between April and August I made major issues getting any meaningful information from most trusted sources, 9/10 times I was just getting someone’s opinion piece or a benchmark would be skewed because the benchmarking software wouldn’t be entirely accurate. This created more stress than necessary for buying one simple computer part and I then decided this project was an opportunity to test my ability on whether or not I can.
